20241020 起

2024-10-20

[photo]

从成都转机曼谷再取道马尼拉回温哥华。辗转腾挪，就是想回去打工。

凌晨四点抵达泰国之后，又等到了早十一点，结果查验履行文件的菲律宾航空人员告诉我不能登机，让我找加拿大使馆问询。

坐着曼谷复古的巴士，顶着桑拿天的湿热，我就这样被旅行到了曼谷。

距上次，也是第一次，来曼谷，七年了。这次是忐忑带兴奋。

曼谷，不期而遇。

Opening

Transiting from Chengdu to Bangkok, then to Manila, and finally back to Vancouver. Going through all these stops, just to make it back to work.

Arriving in Thailand at 4 a.m., I waited until 11 a.m., only to be told by a Philippine Airlines staff member checking travel documents that I couldn't board and had to consult the Canadian embassy.

Riding Bangkok's vintage buses under the sweltering heat, I ended up “traveling” through Bangkok this way.

It’s been seven years since the last time—and also the first time—I came to Bangkok. This time, it’s a mix of anxiety and excitement.

Bangkok, an unexpected encounter.
