20241021 摩的

2024-10-21

[photo]

今年早些时候，同样的文件，我去了墨西哥，也飞了瑞士。只觉得这趟曼谷之行只是小插曲，跟相关部门沟通好了，自然可以成行。

来到加拿大驻曼谷使馆，一通交涉后，他们让我上IRCC的网上填表。看来事情并不是我想象得那么简单。

决定坐摩的回青旅。大热天的，骑手小哥倒是头盔啥的全副武装，我背个包重心靠后；抱他腰岂是直男行为？！我双手握紧后座，生怕牛顿老爷子把我带走。

总体心情不差，感谢命运馈赠的假期。

Motorbike

Earlier this year, with the same documents, I traveled to Mexico and Switzerland without issue. I thought this Bangkok trip would just be a minor detour, and once I communicated with the relevant departments, I’d be on my way.

At the Canadian Embassy in Bangkok, after a round of discussions, they directed me to fill out forms on the IRCC website. It seems the situation isn’t as simple as I’d imagined.

I decided to take a motorcycle taxi back to the hostel. In the sweltering heat, the driver was fully equipped with his helmet and gear, while I leaned back with my heavy backpack. Holding him around the waist—could a straight guy do that?! I gripped the back seat tightly, afraid that Newton himself might take me away (as in, a fatal accident leading to the afterlife).

Overall, my mood is fine, grateful for this unexpected “holiday” gifted by fate.
