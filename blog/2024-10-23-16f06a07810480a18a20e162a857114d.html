20241023 社畜

2024-10-23

[photo]

摊上事儿时，心理会经历五个阶段，直到最终接受现实：该打的工还是要乖乖打。于是在WeWork租了张办公桌，争取工作日可以朝九晚六。

早上从青旅出来走十分钟，搭乘摆渡船走水路在“干净又卫生的”某河过三站，再走十分钟，便到达心仪的打工场所。

在咖啡和啤酒的陪伴下结束一天之后，原路返回，沿路随眼缘觅食。

夜幕拉下，地热散去，青旅的来自第一世界的朋友们就该出去释放荷尔蒙了。逆行的社畜洗洗睡。

WeWork

When facing challenges, the mind goes through five stages, until it finally accepts reality: the work that needs to be done must still be done obediently. So, I rented a desk at WeWork, aiming for a 9-to-6 workday.

In the morning, I leave the hostel, walk for ten minutes, take a ferry along the "clean and hygienic" river, travel three stops, then walk another ten minutes, and finally arrive at my desired workplace.

After ending the day with coffee and beer, I return by the same route, looking for food along the way based on whatever catches my eye.

As night falls and the heat dissipates, friends from the First World at the hostel head out to release their hormones. Meanwhile, the worker heads home, washes up, and goes to sleep.
