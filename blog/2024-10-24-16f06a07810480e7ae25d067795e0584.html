我太喜欢瞎逛了，漫无目的乱走。学术上叫做“随机行走”。或是体质比较吸猫，行走过程中总会遇到各式各样的猫：橘的，狸的，黑的，奶牛，当然也有“煤老板”暹罗猫。

80%情况下，遇到的猫都不惧互动甚至主动来贴贴。

我常会想到醪糟儿。曾经全然了无牵挂的我一去不复返了。再滞留的话，我就真成了错失娃儿童年的不合格父亲了。

曾经用于哄妈的“练习带娃儿”竟一语成谶。

Cats

I really enjoy aimlessly wandering around, strolling without any particular destination. Academically, it's called "random walk." Or perhaps it's because I'm naturally inclined to attract cats, as during my walks, I always encounter all kinds of cats: orange ones, tabbies, black ones, cows, and of course, the "coal baron" Siamese cats.

In 80% of cases, the cats are not only unafraid of interaction but even actively come over for some affection.

I often think about Laotzur (醪糟儿). The carefree version of myself is long gone and will never return. If I stay any longer, I’ll really become an unqualified father who has missed his child’s formative years.

The "practice of taking care of a child" I once used to placate my mom has, in a way, become a self-fulfilling prophecy.
