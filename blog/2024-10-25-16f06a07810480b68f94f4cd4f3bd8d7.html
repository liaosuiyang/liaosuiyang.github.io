2024-10-25

人生如戏，当你准备好回温哥华踌躇满志的时候，你被定格在曼谷；当你租了办公桌准备打持久战的时候，导演说喝完这杯咖啡开机。

曼谷晚十一点，温哥华早九点。为了随时待命重要邮件，不得不等到凌晨两点再睡。果然，学校得到移民局的消息，说我文件没问题，可以择日返回。

闹着玩儿呢。

Turning point

Life is like a movie. Just when you're ready to return to Vancouver with high hopes, you're frozen in Bangkok. When you've rented a desk and prepared for a long battle, the director tells you to finish this cup of coffee before the cameras roll.

It’s 11 p.m. in Bangkok, and 9 a.m. in Vancouver. To be on standby for important emails, I have to wait until 2 a.m. to sleep. Sure enough, the school received a message from the immigration office saying that my documents are fine, and I can return on a chosen date.

Such a mess.
