20241026 到大城府的火车

2024-10-26

[photo]

周六，睡到自然醒。决定逛下大城府。

坐“二轮全景空调尊享专车”来到火车站，正门不开，被一老太“热情”拦住，说下班火车要等一个小时，坐三小时；包她车，1200泰铢一小时到。我说没事儿，我喜欢坐火车。她没纠缠，并指示我如何去买票。“小费”精神发作，便把兜里的一张二十泰铢给了她。

买了票，十五泰铢，十分钟后发车，车程一个半小时。哎，算了……

三十多度的天，包了浆的复古桑拿小火车，车顶的电扇摇头晃脑，半点顶不上可以拉下来的车窗。

Train to Ayutthaya

On Saturday, I slept until I woke up naturally. I decided to take a trip to Ayutthaya.

I took the “two-wheeled panoramic air-conditioned luxury ride” to the train station. The main gate was closed, and an elderly woman “enthusiastically” stopped me, saying that the next train wouldn’t depart for an hour, and the ride would take three hours. She offered me a car for 1200 baht to get there faster. I told her it’s fine, I enjoy taking the train. She didn’t insist and instead gave me directions on how to buy a ticket. My "tip" spirit kicked in, so I handed her a 20-baht note from my pocket.

I bought a ticket for 15 baht, and the train departed in ten minutes for a journey that would take an hour and a half. Well, never mind...

In the 30+ degree weather, I was on a retro sauna-style little train, with a fan on the roof that wobbled back and forth, barely doing anything against the heat, and the windows that could be lowered provided some relief.
