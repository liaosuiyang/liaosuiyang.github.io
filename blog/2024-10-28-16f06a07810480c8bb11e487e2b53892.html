20241028 猴头

2024-10-28

[photo]

晚上去青旅旁的馆子吃饭，惊奇地发现这家店供着那只“吗喽”。我怀疑过这是哈奴曼，但印度猴不穿金甲不用棒子。我最开始觉得新奇，又觉得搞笑，最后觉得小丑是自己。

信啥是别人的事，只要人家可以从信仰中得到慰藉，获得能量，信仰便获得了其存在的意义。

从这个角度上讲，佛头与猴头，并无高下。

Wukong

In the evening, I went to a restaurant next to the hostel and was surprised to find that the place had a statue of that 'monkey.' I wondered if it was Hanuman, but the Indian monkey doesn’t wear golden armor or carry a staff. At first, I thought it was novel, then amusing, and in the end, I realized the clown was me.

What people believe in is their business. As long as their faith brings them comfort and energy, faith has already found its purpose.

From this perspective, there is no distinction between Buddha head and Wukong.
