20241029 露天铁馆

2024-10-29

[photo]

13.749984N, 100.50261E

离开温哥华后便没有撸过铁，加上曼谷这凭空多出来的“假期”，快一个月了。瞎逛的时候，经过公园里的这家铁馆。三十泰铢一次，原汁原味的铁。在露天的公园里，近三十度的桑拿天里。满头的大汗，你不知道是练出来的，还是蒸出来的，但大概都有。

运营铁管的是一家人。老爷子非常健壮，大小臂令人艳羡；缅甸汉子看我新来的，我稍有动作，他便起身想来指导；他夫人则在一旁练习并收钱；三个人轮流照看婴儿车。

我是不讲究器材的。氛围好了就是精神氮泵。本来说了明天再见，然而却再也没见（换了青旅）。

Open-air gym

After leaving Vancouver, I haven't touched any weights, and with this unexpected 'holiday' in Bangkok, it's been almost a month. While aimlessly wandering, I passed by an outdoor gym in a park. Thirty baht for one session, authentic iron. In an open-air park, on a nearly 30°C sauna day. Sweating profusely, you can't tell if it's from the workout or the steam, but it’s probably a bit of both.

The iron pipes are run by a family. The old man is very strong, with arms that make people envious; a Burmese guy saw me as a newcomer, and as soon as he smelled my confusion, he’d stand up, ready to give guidance; his wife was practicing nearby and collecting the money; the three of them took turns looking after the baby stroller.

I’m not picky about equipment. When the atmosphere is right, it’s a mental boost. I had planned to return tomorrow, but I never saw them again (I switched hostels).
