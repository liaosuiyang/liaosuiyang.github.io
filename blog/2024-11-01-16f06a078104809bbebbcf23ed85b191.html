20241101 失而复得

2024-11-01

到曼谷的第一天，就在兵荒马乱中，和戴了快三年的Apple Watch失散了。我以为只是随手扔进了行李箱，所以过去十多天都没管，直到今天彻查。

抱着侥幸心理问了下上一家青旅，结果真的在那，说是在洗浴间发现的。

失而复得，可能是这只表想呆在室内吹空调吧。

Lost and found

On the first day in Bangkok, amidst the chaos, I got separated from my Apple Watch, which I had been wearing for almost three years. I thought I had just tossed it into my suitcase casually, so I didn’t bother to check for the past ten days, until today when I did a thorough search.

With a bit of hope, I asked the last hostel I stayed at, and it was really there. They found it in the bathroom.

It was lost and then found again. Maybe this watch just wanted to stay indoors and enjoy the air conditioning.
