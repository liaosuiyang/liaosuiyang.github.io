20241102 最后一出

2024-11-02

[photo]

预订了凌晨4:50的车去机场，于是提前一刻钟去卫生间换衣服。一切就绪，才发现洗手间的锁坏了。自己被反锁在内。前台的Nita隔了我三道门，捶门加呼喊一点没用。平时随时带的手机也恰巧放在了大堂充电。

要是车到了我没出现，再没打上车的话，等于是，曼谷这一出还没到完结篇？

好在洗手间的墙没封到天花板，有半米的间隙；好在我不胖；好在平时有撸铁。终于还是翻墙而出。

或许是这最后一波攒齐了业力，或者说人品，值机一切顺利。

天亮前出发，天亮前到达。

The final scene

"I had booked a 4:50 AM ride to the airport, so I went to the bathroom to change clothes a quarter of an hour before. Everything was ready, only to realize the bathroom lock was broken. I was locked inside. The front desk Nita was separated by three doors. I tried pounding and shouting, but to no avail. My digital organ happened to be left charging in the lobby.

If the car arrives and I’m not there, unable to catch the ride, would that mean the Bangkok chapter hasn't reached its finale yet?

Luckily, the bathroom wall wasn’t sealed to the ceiling, leaving a half-meter gap. Luckily, I’m not fat. Luckily, I’ve been working out. In the end, I managed to climb out through the wall.

Maybe this last bit of accumulated karma, or perhaps luck, made everything go smoothly at check-in.

I set off before dawn and arrived before dawn.
