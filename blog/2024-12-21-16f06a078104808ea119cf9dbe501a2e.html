20241221 身份

2024-12-21

偶尔会遇到学术界同行问我training background 之类的问题。作为一个学了两年力学又转了材料学修了个管理学二学位然后在一个超分子化学的课题组读了博，现在在生物化学系搞纳米医学研究的我，多少有些身份焦虑。

搞不清楚。没啥身份。我就是我。

很多概念都是人想出来的，给懒人用的。

不被外界定义，少自己限制自己，才会有更多可能性。

Identity.

I sometimes encounter colleagues in academia asking about my training background and similar questions. As someone who studied mechanics for two years, then switched to materials science, earned a second degree in management, completed a PhD in a supramolecular chemistry lab, and is now doing nanomedicine research in a biochemistry department, I can't help but feel a bit of identity anxiety.

I can't figure it out. I don't really have an identity. I’m just me.

Many concepts are human inventions, created for convenience.

By not letting the outside world define you and limiting yourself less, you open up more possibilities.
