20241222 Malheureusement

2024-12-22

在学习法语的时候，偶然认识了这个单词。常用于拒绝别人的邀约。很少有法语长得这么像德语的。但拆开来，也很好懂。bad hour use。干某件事或见某个人属于不良使用自己时间的范畴。

While learning French, I happened to come across this word. It’s often used to decline someone’s invitation. Rarely do French words look so much like German ones. But when you break it down, it’s quite easy to understand: bad hour use. Doing something or meeting someone falls into the category of a poor use of one’s time.
