20241225 生日

2024-12-25

在人生跨入第三个十年的第一天登陆了加拿大。1228的生日加上一路的漂泊，很少为自己庆祝。受邀去朋友家过圣诞，决定自己带个生日蛋糕给自己庆祝。多少算是耶稣给了我点儿薄面儿。快两年了，一切正常，一切顺利，逐渐成事。

Birthday

On the first day of stepping into my third decade of life, I arrived in Canada. With my birthday on December 28th and a life of constant wandering, I’ve rarely celebrated it for myself. This year, invited to a friend’s house for Christmas, I decided to bring a birthday cake to celebrate myself. In a way, it feels like Jesus was kind enough to share a bit of the spotlight with me.

It’s been nearly two years now. Everything is going smoothly, and I’m gradually making things happen.
