20241228 《5AC》

2024-12-28

在之前一次毫无火花的约会之后，于main st的一家二手书店闲逛并收下这本《The 5 AM Club》。我愿称之为中年人的中二热血鸡汤（非贬义）。只是书中有些虚构的情节多少有点“霸总”的感觉。书的内核不差，不然我也不会去试着身体力行。摘抄些：

P222: leadership on the outside begins within

P246: always remember that the greatest of leaders were all servant leaders

P176: the lifetime habit arc: the trigger — the ritual — the reward — the repetition

Own your morning, elevate your life.

After a sparkless date, I wandered into a secondhand bookstore on Main St. and picked up The 5 AM Club. I’d call it a middle-aged person's version of youthful, passionate self-help hype (no offense intended). However, some of the fictional elements in the book give off a bit of a "dominant CEO" vibe. That said, the book's core ideas aren’t bad—otherwise, I wouldn’t be inspired to try putting them into practice. Here are a few excerpts:

P222：「外在的领导力始于内在。」

P246：「永远记住，最伟大的领导者都是服务型领导者。」

P176：「终生习惯的循环：触发——仪式——奖励——重复。」

掌控清晨，提升人生。
