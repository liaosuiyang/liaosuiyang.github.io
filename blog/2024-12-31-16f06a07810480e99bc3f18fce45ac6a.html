20241231 使命的形式

2024-12-31

深夜独自去wrech beach走阶梯，散步，听播客。听到主播讲不是有了公司之后再去想mission，而是有了mission之后，再形成一家公司。使命的召唤是内核，而公司只是其中一种形式。深以为然。

Late at night, I went alone to Wreck Beach, walked the stairs, and listened to a podcast. The host mentioned that it’s not about starting a company first and then figuring out the mission; rather, it’s about having a mission first, which then gives rise to the company. The call of the mission is the core, and the company is merely one form of it. Agree.
