20250108 发表的义务

2025-01-08

读博期间从一个博后同事听到的一句很中肯的建议：没发表文章的课题几乎等同于没有存在过。这么讲是因为当时有一个很鸡肋的课题在我手里，进退两难。

有些课题做着做着觉得没那么有兴趣了，把手头已有的数据整理，有理有据地攒个故事，给合适的期刊一投，无痛顺产。给纳税人一个交代（至于期刊的paywall又另说）

最近就发了篇这样的，业内还有人讨论。喜闻乐见。虽然有人公开评论说有不完善的地方，我也理解接受。我心里有个“但是”，打个比方吧……

你雇我这个厨子来做烤全羊，我额外把“下水”拿出来多整了个羊杂汤，吃众表示汤有点腥。

今天严重超字数了。

The Obligation to Publish
During my doctoral studies, a postdoctoral colleague shared a piercing insight: a research topic without publication is practically nonexistent. This rings true given my then-current research - a project that seemed frustratingly stuck.
Some research topics naturally lose their initial spark. My approach? Organize existing data, craft a coherent narrative, and submit to an appropriate journal - a painless academic delivery. Consider it a report to taxpayers (journal paywalls aside).
I recently published such a paper, which has sparked some professional discussion. While some public comments pointed out imperfections, I accept them with understanding. Yet, I have a 'however' brewing...
Imagine hiring me as a chef to roast a whole lamb. I then prepare an offal soup as a side dish as extra. If diners find the soup slightly gamey, so be it.
Today, I've significantly exceeded my intended word count.
