20250109 流浪咖啡垫

2025-01-09

[photo]

趁打折入手了胶囊咖啡机，放阳台。12/19手滑，杯垫掉在了三楼阳台。这公寓的安保设置是，你的卡只能刷到你住的那层。无奈我只好贴了个条在电梯，希望三楼的住户能帮忙传达信息。

圣诞期间，楼下邻居外出休假。于是乎，杯垫在经历了近一个月的离家出走和风吹雨打日晒（其实也没啥日晒）之后，与我久别重逢。

Coffee tray odyssey

I bought a capsule coffee machine during a sale and placed it on the balcony. On 12/19, I accidentally dropped my coaster on the third floor balcony. The apartment's security system only allows access to your own floor with your card. Helplessly, I posted a note in the elevator, hoping a third-floor resident would help relay the message.
During the Christmas period, my downstairs neighbor was on vacation. As a result, after nearly a month of being away from home, weathering wind and rain (though hardly any sun), the coaster and I were finally reunited.
