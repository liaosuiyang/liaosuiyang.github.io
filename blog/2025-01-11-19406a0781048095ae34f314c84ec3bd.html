20250111 自由与孤独，陪伴与束缚

2025-01-11

自由的代价/代名词是孤独，陪伴的另一面是束缚。人不能既要又要。选择了自由便难免要承受偶尔的孤独，享受了陪伴就要欣然/坦然接受一定的束缚。

社交媒体多的是人周游了山川河海或是合家团聚却鲜有一个人的孤独空虚或是一家人的一地鸡毛。

命运的馈赠都标好了价。不必要的自由是孤独，不必要的陪伴是束缚。人活到自洽就行，但很难不双标。

Freedom and Solitude, Companionship and Restraint

Freedom comes at the cost of/is synonymous with loneliness, while companionship's other face is constraint. One cannot have it both ways. Choosing freedom inevitably means bearing occasional solitude; enjoying companionship means gracefully/openly accepting certain constraints.
Social media is full of people touring mountains and seas or having family reunions, yet rarely shows one's moments of lonely emptiness or a family's mundane struggles.
Life's gifts all come with a price tag. Unnecessary freedom becomes loneliness, unnecessary companionship becomes constraint. Living in self-consistency is enough, though it's hard not to have double standards.
