20250115 Emotional symphony

2025-01-15

我有一个朋友，跟我说他终于又见到了心仪的姑娘，还一起去看了脱口秀。我说“挺好，有啥进展”。他说“随缘，该做到的做到了，意思也含蓄地表达了，并不是所有事情都要有个结果，发乎情儿止乎礼。”我说“人家又不是中国人，不一定懂你这深意”。他说“那何必跟一个不懂你的人强求呢？智者不入爱河。”“我呸。怂逼。”

A friend told me he finally met the girl he's been interested in again, and they went to a stand-up comedy show together. I asked 'That's nice, any progress?' He replied 'Whatever will be, will be. I did what I should do, expressed my feelings subtly. Not everything needs to have a conclusion - emotions should be expressed but kept within proper bounds.' I said 'She's not Chinese, she might not understand such subtle hints.' He responded 'Then why force things with someone who doesn't understand you? The wise don't fall in love.' 'Pfft. You coward.’
