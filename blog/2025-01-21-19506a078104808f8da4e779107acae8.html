20250121 来都来了

2025-01-21

在中文语境下想说服某人做什么事，“来都来了”绝对是说一不二的金句。在英文语境下，“来都来了”有两种翻译，都很信达雅。

一是“we are only here today”, 非常地“活在当下”；

或者，把“来了”的时间地点扩展一下，当做来到人世间，难得人世间走一遭，那便是YOLO

YOLO

In Chinese contexts, if you want to persuade someone to do something, 'since you're already here' (来都来了) is an absolutely golden, irrefutable phrase. In English contexts, this phrase has two elegant translations:
One is 'we are only here today,' which really captures the sense of 'living in the moment';
Or, if we expand the concept of 'being here' to encompass being in this world, seeing it as a rare journey through life, then it becomes 'YOLO' (You Only Live Once).
