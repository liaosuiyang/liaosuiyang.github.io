20250123 当爹趁早

2025-01-23

偶然被人提醒说，要当爹的话，早一点好，不然年龄差距在那，能量水平啥的大不相同。始吾弗信，直到作为mentor去参加一个活动（其实就是给低年级的本科同学提供职业规划上的帮助），其他mentor（大多是UBC高年级本科生），我一博后确实对低年级的本科生的需求不太了解。

这才对“当爹趁早”有所体会。希望这是过度解读。

Having kids earlier

Someone once reminded me that if I want to be a father, it's better to start earlier - otherwise, the age gap means energy levels and such would be quite different. I didn't believe it at first, until I participated in an activity as a mentor (basically helping lower-year undergrads with career planning). The other mentors (mostly senior UBC undergrads) made me realize that as a postdoc, I truly didn't quite understand the needs of junior undergraduate students.
That's when I began to appreciate the advice about 'having kids earlier.' I hope I'm reading too much into this.
