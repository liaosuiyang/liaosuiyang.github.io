20250125 a sunny day

2025-01-25

在申请fellowship这方面，我称loser第二的话，没人敢称第一。不过写本子这事儿就是慢慢学习修炼的。我脸皮厚，找人求助毫不犹豫。直接写邮件给打过若干次照片的博后S，问她要某个部分的成功范例，结果她毫无保留地把整个本子发给了我。真的感动。

When it comes to fellowship applications, if I claim second place as the biggest loser, no one would dare claim first. However, grant writing is something you learn and improve gradually. I'm thick-skinned and don't hesitate to ask for help. I directly emailed postdoc S, whom I've taken photos with several times, asking for successful examples of a specific section. To my surprise, she sent me her entire grant proposal without reservation. I'm truly touched.
