20250127 清晨，沙滩，朝霞，打渔佬

2025-01-27

城市的心情好的时候，人与之建立连结的话，就可以开启快乐一天。

冬日的温哥华，当这座城市心情好的时候，真的是美得很全面。早上从阳台上看到朝霞，便立刻驱车来到Wreck Beach.几百步的阶梯走下去便是海浪，沙滩，朝霞，打渔佬，和我的好心情。

When a city is in a good mood, if you connect with it, it can unlock a happy day.
Vancouver in winter, when this city is in a good mood, is truly beautiful in every way. When I saw the morning glow from my balcony, I immediately drove to Wreck Beach. A few hundred steps down the stairs led me to waves, sand, dawn's rosy light, fishermen, and my good mood.
