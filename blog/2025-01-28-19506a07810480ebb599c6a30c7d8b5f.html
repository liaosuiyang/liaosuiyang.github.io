20250128 本征动力

2025-01-28

不得不承认，我搞科研有时候还是很“油腻的”。为了驱动他人帮我，我有时会把文章署名轻易挂嘴边（但有一说一，我是说到做到的）。大年初一，在合作者实验室“切肉片”准备“火锅”（cryosectioning tissue）小伙儿非常热心地帮忙，还给我看了他非常酷的小项目。两个人断断续续花了一整天时间去优化“切肉”，跟与同伴玩耍无异。我能感受到他发自内心的想解决问题的动力。有时候，我是不是太狭隘了？

Intrinsic motivation

I have to admit, sometimes I can be quite 'slick' in my research approach. To motivate others to help me, I sometimes casually mention paper authorship (though to be fair, I always follow through on my promises). On Chinese New Year's Day, while 'slicing meat' for the 'hotpot' (cryosectioning tissue) in a collaborator's lab, this young researcher was extremely helpful and even showed me his very cool side project. We spent an entire day, on and off, optimizing the 'meat slicing' process - it felt just like playing with a friend. I could feel his genuine drive to solve problems. Sometimes I wonder, am I being too narrow-minded?
