20250212 利益趋同

2025-02-12

组会上Pieter讲了讲课题组成立近五十年前前后后孵育的公司，以及打算开的公司。打趣地说，每次讲报告都有一大堆利益冲突（conflict of interest）要说明。在场的Pieter的多年好友Jim Russell说，作为临床医生，医院里用的器材等等都是那些善于发现需求并创新的企业家们一点一滴地创造出来的，所以与其说是利益冲突（conflict of interest），我更愿称之为利益趋同（confluence of interest）

我是何其有幸能与他们共事啊！

confluence of interest

During the group meeting, Pieter talked about the companies that were incubated by the research group over its nearly fifty-year history, as well as companies they plan to start. He joked that every time he gives a presentation, there's a long list of conflicts of interest to declare. Pieter's longtime friend Jim Russell, who was present, said that as a clinician, all the equipment used in hospitals was created bit by bit by entrepreneurs who were good at identifying needs and innovating. So rather than calling it a conflict of interest, he prefers to call it a confluence of interest.
How fortunate I am to work with them!
