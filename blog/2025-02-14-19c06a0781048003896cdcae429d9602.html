20250214 人形大语言模型

2025-02-14

终于划掉一件颇具挑战的事：英文授课讲90分钟mRNA疫苗。可能是花了太多时间去备课，反而导致自己越来越紧张。虽然还是讲下来了，但不得不承认其中有20%的片子我是在读AI生成的讲稿。学生的反馈倒是说我的“笑话”讲得不错。感觉自己当了90分钟人形大语言模型，不懂装懂。

是时候讨论一下人工智能时代下，教学模式的范式转移了？

Human-Shaped Large Language Model

Finally crossed off a challenging task: delivering a 90-minute lecture in English on mRNA vaccines. I probably spent too much time preparing, which only made me more nervous. Although I managed to finish the lecture, I have to admit that for about 20% of the slides, I was just reading from an AI-generated script.

Interestingly, the students said they enjoyed my "jokes" the most. Honestly, I felt like a human-shaped large language model for 90 minutes—pretending to know it all.

Isn’t it time we talked about the paradigm shift in teaching models in the era of artificial intelligence?
