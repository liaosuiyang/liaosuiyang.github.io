20250220 海象的屌

2025-02-20

[photo]

省去若干字，总之我来到李俊能教授的办公室，喝茶聊天。教授非常平易近人。他将历年旅行时拍的照片整理出来，旁征博引东西方的金句，并简单配上他自己的人生哲理，形成一本书。说着便要送我一本。还给我展示了海象的“鸡巴骨”，让我拿着给我拍张照哈哈。后来我带着这本书去了乌布，最后把这本书留在了青旅，希望更多的旅人可以看到。

Walrus boner

Skipping some details, I went to Professor CN Lee's office to have tea and chat. The professor was very approachable. He had compiled photos from his travels over the years, cited Eastern and Western quotes, and added his own simple life philosophy to form a book. As he spoke, he offered to give me a copy. He also showed me a walrus penis bone and asked me to hold it for a photo, haha. Later, I took this book to Ubud and eventually left it at the hostel, hoping more travelers would see it.

PS: The term boner as slang comes from the mistaken idea that the penis contains a bone. While most mammals do have a baculum (penis bone), unfortunately humans do not. I don’t know why I feel the need to add “unfortunately”…
