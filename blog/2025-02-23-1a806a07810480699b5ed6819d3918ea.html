新加坡遍地都是规矩，乌布到处都无规矩，人们在混乱中和谐共存。加入摩托大军。租车老登问我“要租小的还是大的”“我自己，小的就行；运气好的话，就两个人（想逗逗他）”。老登笑笑“你没问题的”，随后也很热情地推荐景点，该付钱了，240K，我给了他250K，结果他没零钱直接找了我20K。在路边试骑，路过的美女向我点头致意。老登与我不约而同地对视而笑，“看吧，我说的没错吧。”

Renting a Motorcycle
Singapore is full of rules everywhere, while Ubud has no rules anywhere - people coexist harmoniously in chaos. I join the motorcycle army. The old rental guy asks me, 'Do you want to rent a small one or a big one?' 'Just me alone, so small is fine; if I'm lucky, maybe two people (just teasing him).' The old man smiles, 'You'll be fine,' then enthusiastically recommends tourist spots. When it's time to pay, it's 240K. I give him 250K, but he has no change and just gives me back 20K. While test riding by the roadside, a beautiful woman passing by nods at me. The old man and I spontaneously look at each other and smile. 'See, I told you so.
