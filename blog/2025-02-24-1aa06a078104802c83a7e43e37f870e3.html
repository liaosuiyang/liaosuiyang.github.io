20250224 真诚必杀技

2025-02-24

于路边小摊避瓢泼大雨。店家热情搬来板凳。自觉应该买点东西回馈；没带现金；问可否信用卡？可。那来两包零食。结果低于刷卡阈值。我说那算了。店家执意要送我，我欣然接受。最后我果然照顾他们买了件衣服。结论：真诚是永远的必杀技。

The Killer Move of Sincerity
I took shelter from pouring rain at a small roadside stall. The shopkeeper warmly brought over a stool. Feeling I should buy something in return, but having no cash, I asked if credit card was accepted. Yes. So I chose two snacks. However, the amount was below the card payment threshold. I said never mind then. The shopkeeper insisted on giving them to me for free, which I gratefully accepted. In the end, I did indeed end up buying a piece of clothing from them. Conclusion: Sincerity is the eternal killer move.
