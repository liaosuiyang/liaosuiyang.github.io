20250226 写给自己的话

2025-02-26

[photo]

不要以为来了巴厘，就找到内心的平静了。你只是在一个穷地方短暂地实现了物质生活的自由，从而换取精神世界的喘息。然而，支持你走完一生的，是爱，是热爱。玩归玩，但还是觉得世界太不公平了。希望第一世界多考虑去巴厘“修路”而不是消费。

Words to myself
Don't think that by coming to Bali, you've found inner peace. You've only temporarily achieved material freedom in a poor place, thereby gaining a respite in your spiritual world. However, what supports you throughout your life is love, passion. Having fun is fine, but I still feel the world is too unfair. I hope the First World considers going to Bali to 'build roads' rather than just to consume.
