置顶·为什么要开博客

[photo]

我还是自私，生性凉薄，似乎把很多人和事都看得挺淡。

疫情前每次回国多少要找亲朋好友师长叙旧，疫情三年半没回过加上同龄人似乎都已经成家立业，聊也可能话不投机。终于疫情后回国，第一年（2023）基本就谁也没见；第二年（2024）也是先把自己的形成安排了，剩余三天才发朋友圈……

然后我很感动地被饭局轰炸了，当我以为大家都忘了我时。初中班主任风风火火地从另一个县赶回来，找到了当时的语文数学老师，然后喊了一些同学，凑了一桌。见着了我，拉着我的手就往包间里有说有笑地走。以前玩儿得很好的同学，发小，基友又聚了一桌麻辣烫，喝到半夜，然后是我人生第一次找代驾。那晚，我们又短暂地逃回到十几岁的时候。

可能是性格使然，亦或是年龄到了，也可能是搞学术需要长时间专注，现在也不爱事无巨细地发朋友圈了。经过这次回国，感觉还是有必要给关心我的人一些关心我的机会。于是重拾了多年前的写作习惯，开这么个博客。

写什么呢？

中文都手写，然后敲键盘，主要是感性的东西，反思，复盘，感悟等。

英文就直接是键盘敲出来的，主要是纳米医学领域文章的肤浅评论。

翻译由AI完成，我会大致校读。

短期，或者压根儿不会有公开评论区，因为不想分散太多注意力在这上。而且，写字面向的先是我自己，再是其他人，主打一个“真实”。想聊可以私聊。

Pinned · Why Start a Blog
I'm still selfish and naturally detached, seemingly taking many people and things rather lightly.
Before the pandemic, I would always visit relatives, friends, and teachers whenever I returned to China. After not returning for three and a half years during the pandemic, plus the fact that most peers seem to have settled down with families and careers, conversations might not flow as easily anymore. When I finally returned post-pandemic, I barely met anyone in the first year (2023); in the second year (2024), I only announced my presence on WeChat Moments after arranging my own affairs, with just three days left...
Then I was touchingly bombarded with dinner invitations, just when I thought everyone had forgotten about me. My middle school homeroom teacher rushed back from another county, found my former Chinese and Math teachers, and gathered some classmates for a meal. Upon seeing me, she held my hand and walked cheerfully with me to the private room, chatting and laughing. Then I had another gathering over spicy hotpot with close childhood friends and buddies, drinking until midnight - my first time ever using a designated driver service. That night, we briefly escaped back to our teenage years.
Perhaps it's my personality, or maybe it's age, or possibly the need for extended focus in academic work, but I've lost interest in posting every little detail on WeChat Moments. After this trip home, I felt it necessary to give people who care about me some opportunities to stay connected. So I've picked up my old writing habit from years ago and started this blog.
What will I write about?
Chinese content will be handwritten first, then typed, mainly focusing on emotional content, reflections, reviews, and insights.
English content will be typed directly, mainly my superfacial take on nanomedicine research articles.
Translations will be done by AI, with my general proofreading.
In the short term, or perhaps permanently, there won't be a public comments section, as I don't want to disperse too much attention on this. Besides, I write primarily for myself, then for others, emphasizing 'authenticity.' If you want to discuss, you can message me privately.
That's all.
