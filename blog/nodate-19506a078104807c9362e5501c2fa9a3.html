2025Feb 《How to Prevent the Next Pandemic》 by Bill Gates

二月借上讲mRNA疫苗的课的契机，读完了这本书。第六章是关于疫苗的，结尾处，盖茨谈到mRNA-LNP平台技术要优先优化的几个方向，简直是大白话中的大白话。丝毫不用怀疑盖茨的学术功底，但不是所有会做学术的人都可以“好好说话”。把他的话翻译成学术语言。

In February, taking the opportunity of giving a lecture about mRNA vaccines, I finished reading this book. Chapter 6 is about vaccines. At the end, Gates discusses several priority directions for optimizing mRNA-LNP platform technology in very plain, straightforward language. There's no need to doubt Gates' academic background, but not everyone who can do academic work can 'communicate well.' Let's translate his words into academic language

universal vaccine (multivalency)

one and done (immune memory)

total protection (mucosal immunity)

no more ice chest (formulation stability)

so easy, any one can give it (self administration)
