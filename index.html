About

I'm a postdoctoral fellow at the University of British Columbia, developing lipid nanoparticle (LNP) formulations for mRNA and self-amplifying RNA delivery. My work sits at the interface of polymer/colloid chemistry and nucleic-acid therapeutics — understanding how nanoparticle structure and composition control potency, stability, and biodistribution.

I completed my Ph.D. (D.Sc.) at EPFL in 2022, where I studied single-chain nanoparticles and nanoparticle–protein interactions. Before that, I trained in materials science and management at Tsinghua University.
